THE RHYTHM

Welcome to the game "THE RHYTHM"
I wish you a pleasant time! Find your rhythm and enjoy
Click play to start the game.

  • Press the button when the rhythm-ball is under the button.
  • Depending on the level, for each correct hit you will receive 50 points at the first level, 60 points at the second level and 70 points at the third level.
  • If you press the button too early you will lose 10 points, and if you missed the ball, then -15 points.
  • The higher the level of the game, the higher the speed of movement of the rhythm balls.
