THE RHYTHM

Welcome to the game "THE RHYTHM"
Find your rhythm and enjoy the game!
Press play to start the game.

  • Use arrows to catch the rhythm balls.
  • Depending on the level, for each correct hit you will receive 50 points at the first level, 60 points at the second level and 70 points at the third level.
  • If you press the button too early you lose 10 points, and if you miss the ball you lose 15 points.
  • The higher the level of the game, the higher the speed of the rhythm balls.
